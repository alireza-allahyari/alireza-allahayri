My research

The basic question forming my interest and research activities: What is the correct theory of gravity which best describes the nature from largest to smallest scales, form large scale observations and galaxy surveys to smaller scale objects like black holes. The diversity of problems ready to study needs different analytical and numerical tools I had to master and use. In addition, I try to use every data being based mainly on gravitation or electromagnetic interaction to pin down the theory of gravity. My research involves both theoretical model construction and numerical model comparison with observation. More explicitly, I try to compare theoretical tools and models with the observations by numerical techniques like Bayesian analysis, Fisher matrix approach and mock data. Here I summarize my research experience as well as my future research program.
